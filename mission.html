New Chapter Initiative

Creating a safe path forward for women and children.

New Chapter supports women and children transitioning out of domestic violence through a structured, long-term approach focused on safety, healing, and independence.

Our Mission

We support women and their children transitioning out of domestic violence by providing safe housing, compassionate support, and pathways toward stability and independence.

Our Vision

To create a world where no woman or child remains trapped in domestic violence because they lack a safe place, support, or a path forward.

Our Promise

We believe healing takes more than emergency help. It takes time, community, resources, and a real plan for independence.
