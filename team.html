OUR TEAM

New Chapter Initiative

Meet our team!

New Chapter was built on a simple belief: that no woman should have to face life's hardest moments alone. Our team combines lived experience, professional expertise, and a deep commitment to helping women and children find safety, rebuild confidence, and create lasting change.
This is the wonderful team behind New Chapter Initiative and a brief message from each of them:

Susie Beard - Founder and Board Member

Short Bio: Mother, coach, author, and advocate for women rebuilding their lives after adversity. I believe that with support, healing, and opportunity, every person can create a new chapter.
What Inspires Me: Witnessing the courage it takes to start over and the incredible transformation that follows. I am especially inspired by the opportunity to change the world one life at a time by helping children grow up in safer, healthier environments.
My Hope for the Future: That every woman and child can find safety, healing, and the freedom to create a future filled with hope and possibility. I believe that when we help one child heal, we don't just change a life, we help change the future of our world.

Erin Nordstrom - Board Member


Short Bio: Mother, entrepreneur, and advocate for creating opportunities for people to rebuild their lives. I am passionate about community, connection, and helping others find hope during difficult transitions.
What Inspires Me: Watching people rediscover their strength and realizing that a new chapter is always possible, no matter where their story begins.
My Hope for the Future: That people learn to find happiness within their own minds, heal their hearts, and in doing so, help heal the world. 💜

A message from the Founder:


I am a firm believer that the world can be changed one person at a time. I believe in the goodness of people and in the incredible capacity we all have to rebuild our lives when given the right support, opportunities, and encouragement.
Throughout my life, I have been drawn to helping others recognize their own strength and potential. I believe that lasting change happens when compassion is paired with action, and when people are given the tools they need to create a different future for themselves and their families.

My connection to this mission is deeply personal.
As a survivor of domestic abuse, I understand how difficult it can be to imagine a different life, even when you know your current situation is no longer safe or healthy. One of the greatest barriers to leaving is often not the decision itself, but everything that comes after. Where will I go? How will I support my children? Who will help me while I rebuild?
More than twenty years ago, my best friend and I began dreaming about creating a place that could answer those questions for women facing the same uncertainty. We envisioned a space where women and their children could find safety, support, guidance, and the practical tools needed to create a new beginning.
That dream eventually became New Chapter and Senderos Vida Plena. I joined this work because I believe no woman should feel forced to remain in a situation where she is not treated with love, dignity, and respect simply because she has nowhere else to turn.

I hope to help create a world rooted in greater compassion, empathy, and human connection. A world where we see one another not as labels or circumstances, but as people with dreams, hopes, talents, and the desire to build meaningful lives.
I believe that some of the most powerful change begins with children. Every family we help is about more than one woman rebuilding her life. We are helping shape a different future for her children, and for future generations. We are showing them that healthy relationships, safety, dignity, and opportunity are possible.
My hope is that every person who becomes involved in this mission, whether as a volunteer, donor, partner, or supporter, becomes part of that ripple effect. Together, we have the opportunity not only to change individual lives, but to help break cycles that have existed for generations and replace them with hope, resilience, and possibility.

Susie Beard - Founder of New Chapter Initiative
