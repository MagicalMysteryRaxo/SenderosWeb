New Chapter Initiative

New Chapter Initiative is a non-profit organization born from the need to support women in need.

Every day we hear stories about women being abused and helpless. We believe it doesn't have to be that way.

That is why we started this project. To rescue women and children from those aweful situations and keep them in a safe environment that will give them the tools to be self-sustained and teach them how to help themselves and others around them.

If this is something that calls to you, please, feel free to help us by donating to our project.
