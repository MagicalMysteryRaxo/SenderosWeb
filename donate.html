Help women in need

Help create a safe new chapter for women and children.

Women don't stay in situations of domestic violence because they want to. Many stay because they have nowhere to go.
That's why we're building Senderos Vida Plena in Jalisco, Mexico, a transitional home where women and their children can find safety, support, and a real opportunity to rebuild their lives.
This project is being built by people who believe that every woman deserves the chance to start over.
Your support helps provide safe housing, emotional support, legal guidance, life-skills training, and a path toward independence for families navigating one of the most difficult transitions of their lives.
Whether you choose to donate once, give monthly, share our mission, or simply help us spread the word, you become part of the foundation that makes new beginnings possible.
Help us create a place where a NEW CHAPTER can begin.
