WHAT WE ARE WORKING ON

These are the exciting new things that are coming to this wonderful project:

Right now we are building a safe home where women and their children can start again. Rather than waiting years to build something large, we want to start helping families as soon as possible. We are starting small with this first home. It will provide safety, support, guidance, and the stability needed to begin rebuilding a life free from violence.

the project grows, so will our ability to serve more families. We have already identified a beautiful property in Jalisco, Mexico, where we hope to create a small community of individual homes surrounded by nature. A place where women and children can enjoy privacy, security, and a sense of belonging while being supported by others walking a similar path.

Once we have settled and we have a big enough place and a stable income supply, our vision extends beyond providing shelter. We want to help create opportunities for long-term independence through practical skills. The goal is for them to learn a trade and be able to make jewlery, clothing, journals, tools, and then sell it here in our website and the profit will go 100% to the women who made them.

By helping women build confidence, develop new abilities, and earn their own income, we hope to create a community that is not only safe, but sustainable.
